Business Intelligence Requirements

Regional Sales & Customer Insights System (LENOVO)
Author: AZABE Benine

1. KPIs That Matter

The Business Intelligence (BI) layer focuses on measuring sales performance, customer value, regional trends, payment efficiency, and system operations to support managerial decision-making.

A. Sales Performance KPIs

Total sales revenue per region

Average sales value per transaction

Top-selling laptop or accessory per region

Sales growth rate (weekly / monthly)

Quantity sold per product category (Laptop, Accessory, Spare Part)

B. Payment & Financial KPIs

Payment completion rate (%)

Outstanding balance amount

Percentage of partial and pending payments

Most used payment methods (Cash, Card, Mobile Banking, Online Transfer)

Average payment delay duration

C. Customer & Market KPIs

Top revenue-generating customers

Customer purchase frequency

Sales contribution by customer category (Individual, Retailer, Wholesaler, Corporate)

Customer regional distribution

Customer retention rate

D. System Operations & Audit KPIs

Number of sales transactions per day

Number of payment records per day

Failed or reversed transactions

Daily database activity (inserts, updates, deletes)

Report generation frequency

2. Stakeholders
Stakeholder	Needs
Sales Manager	Monitor regional sales performance and product demand
Finance Manager	Track payments, outstanding balances, and payment methods
BI Analyst	Build dashboards, identify trends, and forecast sales
Executive Management	High-level KPIs and strategic insights
Inventory Manager	Product demand trends to guide stock replenishment
3. Decision Support Needs

Identify high-performing and underperforming regions

Determine which laptop models or accessories are most profitable

Detect customers with high outstanding balances

Analyze payment behavior to reduce credit risk

Support inventory allocation and regional marketing strategies

Evaluate overall business performance for future expansion

4. Reporting Frequency
Report	Frequency
Executive Sales KPI Summary	Weekly
Regional Sales Performance Dashboard	Daily
Payment & Outstanding Balance Report	Daily
Customer Performance Report	Weekly
Product Sales Trend Report	Monthly
5. Required BI Deliverables

Clear KPI definitions and formulas

Interactive BI dashboards (sales, payments, customers, regions)

Analytical SQL queries for KPI computation

Executive summary visualizations for management review

MIS & BI Alignment (Short Justification)

The BI layer transforms transactional sales and payment data from the PL/SQL system into actionable insights. By analyzing regional performance, customer behavior, and payment trends, the system enables LENOVO management to make informed decisions regarding inventory management, financial control, marketing strategies, and long-term business planning.
